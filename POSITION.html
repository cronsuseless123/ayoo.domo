A LEADER'S POSITION

It’s not about the power or fame,

It’s the quiet strength behind the name.

A leader’s position, unseen, it’s true,

Lies in the service they give to you.

 

It’s guiding hands that lead the way,

In moments of doubt, they choose to stay.

A position, yes, it can be claimed,

But true leadership is never the same.

 

It’s the heart that leads, not the seat,

The courage to make decisions neat.

For in the role, there’s more to see

The strength of leadership, humbly free.

 

Position is not the leader’s gain,

It’s the responsibility, the strain.

For those who lead with heart and mind,

Know their true position is in what they find.
